ABOUT ME

Being a photographer is totally awesome. There are many reasons why but I will share with you my top 3 or 3 reasons why I am a photographer.


First, I wanted to capture a memory that I can have forever. In the case of my only son and only child whom my wife and I were blessed after eight long years, to capture the memories of Auric's growing years through photography became a passion. Every smile, every tear, every laughter, at play or asleep, all these and more is a moment that will pass but will forever be retained.


Secondly, I appreciate the beauty that is all around me. Everyday is filled with it in the most ordinary or unexpected places, from the most simple creation of God to the most complex ones. It is the awesomeness and greatness of God that has inspired me to capture his work of art.


Thirdly, I lose all my worries. Photography is something you get immersed in. I loose myself in shooting and all my worries and daily stresses melt away with every photo that everytime I hold my camera, it excites my whole being and there is joy in the thought that I have accomplished something that I can share.
